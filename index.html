Welcome

With 19 years of Academic Excellency….. The Talent school is a well known educational institution established in Sathupally, Khammam dist, Telangana. Talent school has already set a benchmark in academic excellence by continuously delivering top and matchless results in Intra and State competitive examinations.

Who we are

  • Mission

    The world today is a global village and people are its citizens. As boundaries of location, people and time cease to exist, it is of utmost importance that we move with the times. At Talent School, we have created a unique blend of curricula, contemporary teaching methodologies, and equal focus on intellectual, physical and personality development, resulting future leaders who are ready to take on the world. Today, we stand as the force behind creating countless doctors, engineers, IAS officers, chartered accountants, and so much more..

  • Vision

    We endeavour to be the right mentor for IIT-JEE aspirants, while also helping them develop a global perspective, with a keen interest in community life. We prepare students for competitive exams by providing in-depth knowledge of various subjects at an intermediate level. The Talent School is committed to instill well-rounded academic rigor and imbue the zeal for competition so that one's course of action can lead them to reach the zenith of success meaningfully.

  • Management

    Puli Srinivasa Rao is regarded as one of the pioneering forces in teaching and learning. In recent decades, academicians and educationists have been able to bring a striking shift from inaccessible and archaic academic setups in Telangana schools and colleges. Puli Srinivasa Rao's commitment to qualitatively change the previous scenario into a dynamic and systematic learning environment has, in fact, provided the impetus for this shift in Telangana and beyond.
